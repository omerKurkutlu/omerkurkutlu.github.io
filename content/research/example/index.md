My research focuses on developing intelligent autonomous robotic systems capable of operating safely and efficiently in complex real-world environments. I am particularly interested in perception-driven autonomy for resource-constrained robots, combining computer vision, deep reinforcement learning, and embedded AI.

My current research directions include:

- **Vision-Based Autonomous Navigation** using monocular cameras
- **Tiny Drone Autonomy** for indoor and GPS-denied environments
- **Vision-Language-Action (VLA) Models** for robotic navigation
- **Deep Reinforcement Learning** for obstacle avoidance and motion planning
- **TinyML and Edge AI** for battery-powered and batteryless devices
- **Simulation-to-Real Transfer** using ROS, Gazebo, Isaac Sim, and Webots

My long-term goal is to develop intelligent robotic systems that can perceive, reason, and navigate autonomously while operating under strict computational and energy constraints.
